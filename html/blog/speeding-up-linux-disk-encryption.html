Speeding up Linux disk encryption

By Zac Szewczyk on 2020/03/31 10:48:47 EST

Ignat Korchagin posted a fantastic writeup on speeding up Linux disk encryption to Cloudflare’s blog. The lengths that team went to in order to solve this problem are fascinating. Dan Luu hit the nail on the head when he highlighted Cloudflare’s as an example of a great corporate blog.

For those looking for more interesting reads, Dan highlighted a few other corporate engineering blogs in his post; start there. I have read several [interesting](https://labs.ripe.net/Members/philip_homburg/whats-the-deal-with-ipv6-link-local-addresses

) posts from RIPE Labs over the last few weeks as well. Although not a corporation, few organizations operate at that level and scale. You may also find some interesting sources in the list of websites I use to keep up with current events, or the list of (mostly) independent shops whose RSS feeds I subscribe to.
