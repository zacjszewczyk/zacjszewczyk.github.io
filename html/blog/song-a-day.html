Jonathan Mann's Song a Day

By Zac Szewczyk on 2013/5/29 19:33:7 EST

Since January first, 2009, Jonathan Mann has written a song every day. Every single day, for four years, four months, and four weeks exactly, Jonathan Mann, without fail, wrote and produced a song. That’s 1609 days, and 1609 songs. Those who have a hard time writing every day, feel free to take notes.

His song for Hypercritical, #1450, and what became the Accidental Tech Podcast’s ending theme, #1546, are my two favorites. If you’re looking for some background, check out the bio video he posted last year; otherwise, go check out some of his songs.
