A Note on AT&T's 5G Network

By Zac Szewczyk on 2020/05/17 07:44:07 EST

With all the talk of Huawei over the last few days, I want to spend a minute on AT&T. You may have seen the company in the news for expanding 5G to new cities despite poor performance. In short, AT&T markets an optimized 4G LTE network as 5GE. Even if AT&T did offer actual 5G, no U.S. devices could use it; it does not. Huawei receives so much attention in this space because it is the first company to offer an actual 5G network, a feat no one else can match.
