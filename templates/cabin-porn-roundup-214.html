Cabin Porn Roundup

By Zac J. Szewczyk on 2014/03/01 07:29:34

I have a very light issue for you this month, unfortunately: precious little caught my eye over the past few weeks. In fact, just two articles did, and only one came from Cabin Porn: a third-generation hand-made cabin from Michigan. This structure, to me, defines the genre: built of wood and stone and out in the middle of the woods, not some modern architecture retrofitted into a rustic getaway a few hundred feet from the nearest road, this cabin has character. And I love that about it.

The second and final link comes from Vimeo, by way of Twitter: Zen and the Art of Powder Boarding. Although I’m not convinced the video’s creators did not just prepend the title with “zen” in an attempt at making it somehow more attractive to potential viewers, I did find myself enjoying this short view into the world of powder boarding. I’m no snowboarder — barely a skier — but even if just to visit all the neat places filmed here, I would love to become one.
