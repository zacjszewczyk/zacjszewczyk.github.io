The Importance of Proper Trailer Loading

By Zac J. Szewczyk on 2019/10/03 07:26:12 EST

I see U-Haul’s video on the importance of proper trailer loading resurface every once in a while, and most recently at Jalopnik. I do my best to avoid trailers. I don’t even like pulling them on paved roads, and off-road, they make all but the easiest tracks impossible. If you get stuck using one, though, at least know how to load it.
